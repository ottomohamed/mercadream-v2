Frequently Asked

What counts as a generation?▼
One complete render of a video. Edits and re-renders count as separate generations. Preview renders at reduced quality do not count.
Can I switch plans anytime?▼
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is my content used for AI training?▼
Never. Your content is encrypted and never used to train shared models. Your intellectual property remains yours.
Which video platforms does the output work with?▼
Our prompts are optimized for Runway Gen-3, Kling 1.6, Pika 2.0, and Sora. We update our output engine as new platforms emerge.
Do unused videos roll over?▼
Unused generations do not roll over on Regular and Advanced plans. Professional plan has unlimited generations.
