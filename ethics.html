Creating Without Compromising.

MercaDream believes creative freedom and ethical responsibility are inseparable. This document outlines our commitments to you, to creators everywhere, and to the world our technology shapes.

⟳ LAST UPDATED: JUNE 2025 · VERSION 3.1
OUR MISSION

MercaDream was built on a single conviction: every person deserves the ability to bring their imagination to life in cinematic form, regardless of budget, technical skill, or access to production resources.

We believe AI-powered creation should expand human creativity — not replace it, not homogenize it, and never weaponize it. Our technology is designed to serve the creator's vision, not redirect it.

This mission requires discipline. Not every use of our technology is one we will support. The commitments below are not aspirations — they are enforced policies that shape every product decision we make.

OUR COMMITMENTS
🔒
YOUR DATA IS YOURS
Your creative content, voice data, and prompts are never used to train shared models. What you create belongs entirely to you.
🌐
CULTURAL RESPECT
MercaDream is natively trilingual and designed with cultural sensitivity. We do not produce content that degrades, stereotypes, or misrepresents any culture or community.
💎
TRANSPARENT PRICING
No hidden fees, no surprise charges. You know exactly what you pay for and what you get. Our billing is public and auditable.
🛡
SAFETY BY DEFAULT
Safety filters are always active. They cannot be disabled by users, API callers, or operators. No exceptions, no workarounds.
🔏
AES-256 ENCRYPTION
All content is encrypted at rest and in transit. Multi-region replication with zero-knowledge architecture.
⚖
CREATOR SOVEREIGNTY
We will never claim ownership of your creations, license them to third parties, or display them publicly without your explicit consent.
CONTENT POLICY

MercaDream operates under a strict content policy enforced at the model, API, and platform levels simultaneously. Our Dream Extractor model is fine-tuned to refuse harmful requests regardless of how they are phrased.

⚠ STRICTLY PROHIBITED
Sexual content involving minors (CSAM) — zero tolerance, always reported to NCMEC
Non-consensual deepfakes or synthetic media depicting real individuals without documented consent
Content designed to incite violence, terrorism, or hatred against protected groups
Disinformation, electoral manipulation, or synthetic propaganda
Impersonation of real public figures in harmful, defamatory, or deceptive contexts
Content designed to facilitate harassment, stalking, or threats
✓ SUPPORTED USE CASES
Fiction, storytelling, and cinematic art of all genres and themes
Commercial video production, advertising, and brand content
Educational and documentary-style content
Music videos, artistic experimentation, and avant-garde projects
Satire and commentary on public figures within legal bounds
Historical recreation and cultural preservation projects

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Content you generate using MercaDream is yours. You own the copyright to your outputs to the extent permitted by law in your jurisdiction. MercaDream does not claim any ownership, license, or rights over generated content.

You grant MercaDream a limited, non-exclusive license solely to store and deliver your content back to you. We do not sublicense, display, or monetize your content in any form.

MercaDream's own training data consists exclusively of properly licensed corpora. We respect copyright holders and operate in full compliance with applicable intellectual property law.

All output content is watermarked with a C2PA-compliant AI provenance signal embedded invisibly in the media. This protects both creators (proof of origin) and the public (transparency about AI generation).

RESPONSIBLE AI

MercaDream operates under a continuous AI safety program. Our red-teaming team probes the model quarterly for new failure modes, bias patterns, and policy circumvention techniques.

We publish a semi-annual safety report disclosing: model refusal rates by category, bias evaluation results, content moderation accuracy, and known limitations. These reports are public and linked below.

MercaDream participates in the Partnership on AI, the Content Authenticity Initiative (CAI), and the Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity (C2PA). We hold ourselves accountable to external standards, not just internal ones.

TRANSPARENCY

Every video generated by MercaDream contains a C2PA watermark identifying it as AI-generated content. This watermark survives re-encoding, social media compression, and format conversion. We believe AI-generated media should be identifiable as such.

We publish the following on a rolling basis: infrastructure uptime and incident reports, quarterly safety evaluations, data breach notifications (within 72 hours of discovery), and material changes to this policy (30 days advance notice).

If we materially change how we handle your data or generate your content, you will receive direct notification and the opportunity to export your data before changes take effect.
